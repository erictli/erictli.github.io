Helping Media Companies Search for Real-Time Social Content

Overview

Capture helps brands and publishers find, display, and license real-time social content. Our customers included CNN, Buzzfeed, The Washington Post, and The Weather Channel. During my time at Capture, we redesigned our core product (which allowed customers to search and acquire rights for social photos and videos in real-time), launched a self-service sign-up and payments flow, and built new features for e-commerce and brand marketing customers. We also concepted, designed, and pitched an on-demand video product that made it easy for publishers to get on-demand video coverage of breaking news events. As the only designer, I also helped create prototypes and presentations for partnership discussions, which ultimately resulted in Capture being acquired.
