Topology

Along with a friend, I launched Topology, a direct-to-consumer brand that makes button-down shirts in the USA using unique Japanese fabrics. With no experience in fashion design or manufacturing, we sourced fabrics, found a manufacturer, and created 20 prototype shirts. After selling those in a of couple weeks, we decided to launch a full collection. We conducted customer research, figured out our supply chain, built an e-commerce site, took product and lifestyle photos, and launched and marketed the brand. A few hundred shirts later, we continue to run Topology, you can visit us here or read more about our journey here.
