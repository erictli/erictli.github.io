About Me

I grew up in the Midwest and studied economics at the University of Chicago before jumping into a career in finance in New York City. After a few hundred hours of staring at Excel models, I realized that I wanted to solve more interesting problems and discovered the world of product design (with a lot of learning along the way). Since then, I’ve been lucky enough to work on interesting problems at companies ranging from 10 to 100,000+ employees.

If you'd like to get in touch, feel free to email me at hi@ericli.io.
