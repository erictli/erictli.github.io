The Appendix

Here's some additional work - usually either work-in-progress or projects that were put on hold before they were polished.

Deal Checker (WIP)

A feature that helps users find the best price on whatever they are buying. Users share any product URL and get a recommendation for how they can get the best deal on that product, factoring coupons and other savings. Users submit deals on a website and receive their deal via email within 24 hours.



Shopbolt Universal Checkout (WIP)

A browser extension that lets users easily checkout on any e-commerce site with a credit card and address saved to their Shopbolt account. Users don't need to share credit cards with new retailers or fill out multiple forms for multiple sites."



Mobile Points Events (Concept)

Concept of mobile gamification of points in a mobile rewards app to help onboard users and encourage repeat engagement.



Local Rewards (Concept)

Concept of a flow where users can earn and spend rewards points at local stores that they shop at frequently (here, focused on gas stations). The challenge here was walking users through the pump experience where we had no control over the UX. Note: visual design not complete.



Crowdsourced Deals for New Parents (Concept)

Concept of a product and deal-community for new parents to discover the best deals on the products they buy most. A responsive web app that highlighted deals by relevancy, surfaced key categories, and emphasized community discussion.
