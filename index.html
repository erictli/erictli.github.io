A bit more about me.

I'm an investment banker turned designer based in New York City. I work on the Uber Eats Marketplace Team where I lead logistics, predictions, and pricing projects across our consumer, restaurant, and courier apps.

I studied economics at UChicago and covered tech and media companies at J.P. Morgan. After growing tired of staring at spreadsheets all day (and night), I led an innovation-focused product team at Sears. I then joined Capture, where I designed social-search tools used by CNN and The Washington Post. After Capture, I led product design at Bread where I designed easy-to-use financial products and helped the company grow from less than 20 to over 100 people.
