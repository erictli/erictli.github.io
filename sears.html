Sears Holdings

Sears Holdings was looking to grow its rewards program to more broadly impact its customers' lives. I helped identify user needs and design and build new web and mobile products.

Sears Holdings was focused on growing its rewards program to provide value across its customers' lives, wherever and whenever they shopped. I helped conduct research and interviews that identified that users cared most about points and coupons and struggled to understand the existing rewards program. I then built and lead a team to create new web and mobile experiences focused on simplifying the program and helping members save money.

I directly contributed to the design and ideation of a new mobile-application aimed at helping users save money by earning points and finding coupons wherever they shop. I did everything from designing and pitching concepts, to developing detailed UX flows and working with other designers to design mobile interfaces. In addition to contributing design, I lead a product team, managed partner and agency relationships, and helped conceive and execute labs experiments in local fulfillment and conversational commerce.
